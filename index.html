Beagles: The Friendly Hound

Beagles are a small scent hound, typically no taller than 13 inches at the shoulder. They are known for their distinctive baying howl, friendly temperament, and remarkable sense of smell.

Beagle Trivia

  1. Beagles were originally bred for hunting rabbits.
  2. Beagles have been bred for over 2,000 years.
  3. They are one of the most popular dog breeds in the world.
  4. Beagles were used by Snoopy in the Peanuts comic strip.
  5. Their sense of smell is so strong, they can sniff out contraband at airports and border crossings.
